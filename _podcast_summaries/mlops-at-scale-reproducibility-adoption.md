# MLOps at Scale: CI/CD, Reproducibility, Model Monitoring & Adoption Strategies

## Why This Episode Matters

This episode focuses on MLOps as an enabling team function, not just a toolbox. It is strong on adoption strategy, feedback loops, quick wins, reproducibility, monitoring, and the skill mix needed to support product teams. It is useful for articles that need to explain why MLOps succeeds or fails inside organizations.
