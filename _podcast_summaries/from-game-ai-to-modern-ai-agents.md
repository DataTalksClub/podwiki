# From Game AI to LLM Agents: 20-Year Evolution of Multi-Agent Systems, Evolutionary Algorithms & Modern AI Tooling

## Why This Episode Matters

This episode connects older game AI, reinforcement learning, evolutionary algorithms, and modern LLM agents. It is useful for agent-oriented research because it treats agents as part of a longer technical lineage rather than a brand-new product category. It also includes practical discussion of workflow design, orchestration, tool integration, local models, and evaluation.
