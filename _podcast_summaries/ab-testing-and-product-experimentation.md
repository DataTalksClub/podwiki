# Product Analytics & A/B Testing: Causality, Metrics, Power Analysis, A/A Tests

## Why This Episode Matters

This is the strongest archive candidate for experimentation fundamentals. It explains A/B testing through causality, metric design, randomization, power analysis, statistical tests, p-values, Bayesian alternatives, and multiple comparisons. It is useful whenever product analytics or data product validation needs transcript-backed examples.
