# Nasser Qadri

## Background

Nasser Qadri is an AI Engineer at Google. Previously, he held data science leadership roles at Quest Diagnostics, Deloitte, and Gallup. Before moving into industry, Nasser completed a PhD in Politics and International Relations at the University of Glasgow. His background combines social science research, data science, and applied machine learning.
