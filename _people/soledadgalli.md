# Soledad Galli

## Background

Soledad Galli is a lead data scientist and founder of [Train in Data](https://www.trainindata.com/){:target="_blank"}. She developed machine learning models in finance and insurance, received a Data Science Leaders Award in 2018 and was selected “LinkedIn’s voice” in data science and analytics in 2019.
