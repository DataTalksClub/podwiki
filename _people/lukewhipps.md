## Background

Luke Whipps is a co-founder of Neural.AI, a recruiting company focused on data scientists and AI professionals. His source bio also notes his experience as a recruiter and host of the AI Game Changer podcast.

## Podcast Contributions

- [Land Data Scientist Roles](https://datatalks.club/podcast.html) documents recruiter-side advice on resumes, portfolios, interviews, negotiation, and outreach.
- [Master Machine Learning and Data Science Interviews](https://datatalks.club/podcast.html) extends that hiring perspective into interview stages, candidate signals, and preparation strategy.
