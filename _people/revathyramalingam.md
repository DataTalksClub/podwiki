# Revathy Ramalingam

## Background

Revathy Ramalingam is a Senior Software Engineer and AI Engineer at a healthcare startup. With over nine years of prior experience in software development in the telecom domain and a subsequent seven-year career break, she successfully transitioned back into the industry by upskilling through ML Zoomcamp, AI Dev Tools Zoomcamp, and the AI Hero email course at DataTalks.Club. She will share the strategies, interview insights, and lessons that helped her rebuild her career step by step.
