# Rosona Eldred

## Background

Rosona Eldred is a trained mathematician working in the “data space” for the last 6 years, the last three with industrial data. She is currently a machine learning engineer with a technical leadership role around synthetic tabular data in an AI Innovation team. She’s particularly intrigued by Industrial R&D problems, especially around dealing with tiny data.
