## Background

Hugo Bowne-Anderson is Head of Developer Relations at Outerbounds and host of Vanishing Gradients. His source bio describes experience across data science, education, developer evangelism, content marketing, and data strategy consulting.

## Podcast Contributions

- [DevRel Role for Machine Learning](https://datatalks.club/podcast.html) explores ML ecosystems, open-source governance, and developer relations as a technical and community function.
- [Practical LLM Engineering and RAG](https://datatalks.club/podcast.html) adds concrete LLM engineering patterns around prompting, evaluation, retrieval, and real-world workflows.
