## Background

Barr Moses is CEO and co-founder of Monte Carlo. Her source bio includes prior work scaling customer operations at Gainsight, consulting at Bain, and statistics research at Stanford.

## Podcast Contributions

- [Data Observability Explained](https://datatalks.club/podcast.html) contributes the archive's core framing for data observability, including freshness, lineage, schema detection, downtime prevention, drift, and false positives.
