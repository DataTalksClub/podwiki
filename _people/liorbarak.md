# Lior Barak

## Background

Lior Barak is the author of "Data is Like a Plate of Hummus" a book about setting a data strategy for non-business functions, the co-host of "WHAT the Data?!" Podcast talking about data and business growth, and the founder of Tale About Data.

Discussed: Episode Theme: Bridging Data & Management; Guest Background: Lior''s data and product journey; Role Defined: Data strategist as translator between business and tech; Communication Tactics: Proactive alerts to maintain data trust.
