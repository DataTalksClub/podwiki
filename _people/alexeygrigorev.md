## Background

Alexey Grigorev is the founder of DataTalks.Club. In the podcast archive he appears both as host and as a subject-matter contributor on how the community, courses, and practical data education have evolved.

## Podcast Contributions

- [Data Team Roles Explained](https://datatalks.club/podcast/data-team-roles.html) frames data work as a collaboration between data analysis, data engineering, machine learning, MLOps, product management, and leadership.
- [CRISP-DM Methodology for Data Science Projects](https://datatalks.club/podcast/crisp-dm.html) gives the archive an early reference point for project structure, business understanding, modeling, evaluation, and deployment.
- [DataTalks.Club Behind the Scenes](https://datatalks.club/podcast/datatalksclub-building-scaling-data-community.html) and [Building a Sustainable Data Community](https://datatalks.club/podcast/datatalksclub-building-sustainable-data-community-3-years-anniversary.html) document the operational side of growing a technical learning community.
- [Inside Scaling DataTalks.Club](https://datatalks.club/podcast/datatalksclub-scaling-and-free-courses.html) connects the free Data Engineering, MLOps, and LLM courses to community growth and career transition pathways.

## Concepts Connected

- [community building]({{ '/wiki/community-building/' | relative_url }}) as a durable operating model for meetups, courses, and peer learning.
- [data engineering]({{ '/wiki/data-engineering/' | relative_url }}) and [MLOps]({{ '/wiki/mlops/' | relative_url }}) as recurring course and podcast pillars.
