# Radovan Bacovic

## Background

Radovan Bacovic is a Staff Data Engineer who lives, enjoys, and codes in Novi Sad, Serbia. Part of the Data giants ambassador program: Snowflake Squad member and dbt spotlight member. An experienced data engineer and “wannabe” the best bad conference speaker. He is always eager to discover new data technologies in a rapidly changing environment. He has a profound background in application development at large international companies worldwide. He strongly advocates for the open-source community and the open-core approach.
